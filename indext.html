Team Plan

Player Roles Goalkeeper (GK) The goalkeeper protects the goal and stops the opposing team from scoring. Center Back (CB) Strong defenders who block attacks and clear the ball from danger areas. Full Back (LB/RB) Defenders who protect the sides of the field and support attacking plays. Defensive Midfielder (CDM) Helps the defense by winning the ball and stopping opponent attacks. Central Midfielder (CM) Controls the game by passing, creating chances, and supporting both defense and attack. Attacking Midfielder (CAM) Creates scoring opportunities and supports the forwards in attack. Winger (LW/RW) Fast players who attack from the sides and deliver crosses or score goals. Striker (ST) Main goal scorer of the team, focused on finishing attacking chances. Captain Leads and motivates the team during matches and training sessions.
